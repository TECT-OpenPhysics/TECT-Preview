\subsection*{0. Summary}

Implementing the operator's specification verbatim: the uniform variance
assumption $\sigma(x) \to M$ is replaced by the exact Bloch Green diagonal
\[
\sigma_A(x) = G_{\phi_A}(x,x), \qquad
\hat\sigma(Q) = \frac{1}{v_{\rm cell} N_k}\sum_k \sum_G
\big[K^{(k)}\big]^{-1}_{G,\,G+Q},
\]
and the exact Wick remainder is used in the Gibbs--Bogoliubov functional
\[
F_{\rm var}/V = F_{\rm cl}(A) + \tfrac12\,\overline{\mathrm{Tr}\ln K}
- \tfrac12(3uM{+}15vM^2)\,\bar\sigma - 15vM\,\overline{\phi_A^2\sigma}
+ \tfrac{3u}{4}\,\overline{\sigma^2} + \tfrac{15v}{2}\,\overline{\phi_A^2\sigma^2}
+ \tfrac{5v}{2}\,\overline{\sigma^3},
\]
with all cell averages computed exactly (band-limited $48^3$ grid;
$\hat\sigma(Q)$ from per-$k$ inversion of the full Bloch matrix). With
this remainder $F_{\rm var}(A,M)$ is a TRUE variational upper bound for
every $(A,M)$, and at $A = 0$ its $M$-stationarity reproduces the
disordered gap equation, so the Reading-H reference is the $A{=}0$
optimum of the SAME functional (implementation identity exact $=$
diagonal at $W{=}0$: $10^{-10}$; continuum reference zero and $M$-optimal
at $M_R$: asserted).

\subsection*{1. Autopsy of the uniform-Wick $-0.102$}

At the old artefact point $(A, M) = (0.114, 1.6\,M_R)$ the exact-Wick
values are $+0.060$ (in-basis) and $+0.092$ (anchored) --- an inversion
of $\sim 0.16$. Mechanism: the exact remainder uses the ACTUAL trial
variance, and $\bar\sigma$ DECREASES monotonically with $M$ (e.g.\ at
$A = 0.06$: $\bar\sigma = 0.042 \to 0.014$ as $M: M_R \to 2.2 M_R$),
because the kernel mass $m_H^2(M) = r + 3uM + 15vM^2$ stiffens. The
uniform-Wick counterterm instead substituted the scan parameter $M$
itself, producing the spurious $-5vM^3 - 15vnA^2M^2$ runaway that
manufactured the boundary negative. G1$''$-1$'$ thereby CONFIRMS the
Math428 classification of $-0.102$ as a counterterm-validity artefact ---
now by direct computation rather than interpretation.

\subsection*{2. Method (anchoring, reference-free bracket)}

$\Delta F_{\rm anchored}(A,M) = \Delta F^{\rm cont}_{\rm diag}(A,M)
+ \big[F^{\rm exact}_{\rm basis}(A,M) - F^{\rm diag}_{\rm basis}(A,M)\big]$:
the continuum diagonal-limit closed form (radial integrals; reference
$(0, M_R)$ built in, $= 0$ there) plus the in-basis off-diagonal $+$
$\sigma$-inhomogeneity bracket, which needs NO reference subtraction
because exact $\equiv$ diagonal at $A = 0$ identically. This extends the
Math428 anchoring protocol to the exact-Wick functional; the basis-trace
quadrature shift (the in-basis $A{=}0$ optimum sits below $M_R$;
recorded) affects only the diagnostic in-basis race, not the anchored
construction.
