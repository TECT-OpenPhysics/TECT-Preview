$F_{\rm var} = F_{\rm cl} + \tfrac12\mathrm{Tr}\ln\hat K + \langle H - H_{\rm trial}\rangle$;
in the uniform-variance (Hartree) treatment $\sigma(x) \to M$ the remainder
evaluates to $C_{\rm unif}$ above. Substituting the disordered gap relation

exactly the isotropic ordered kernel ($p_2(0) = 2n$, $p_4(0) = N_4$), so
the OFF-DIAGONAL share is cleanly isolated as
$\Delta F_{\rm full} - \Delta F_{\rm diag}$.

\subsection*{3bis. Analytic amplitude bounds (v1.1; operator-audit weakness 3)}

\paragraph{Small $A$ (closes $A < 0.02$).} Expanding the anchored
estimator at $A \to 0$: $\hat r(0) = m_H^2(M_R) = r + 3uM_R + 15vM_R^2
= r_R$ and $d\hat r/dA^2|_0 = 2n(3u + 30vM_R)$, so
\[
\Delta F_{\rm est}(A) = c_2 A^2 + O(A^4),\qquad
c_2 = n\big[r + 3uM_R + 30vM_R^2 - 15vM_R^2\big] = n\, r_R
= 6 \times 0.304526 = 1.8272 \;>\; 0 ,
\]
exactly (the off-diagonal share is $O(A^4)$: $W \propto A^2$). Numerical
cross-check: $\Delta F_{\rm est}(0.02)/A^2 = 1.815$ ($0.7\%$ from
$c_2$; scripted claim). The region $A < 0.02$ is therefore closed
analytically with positive margin.

\paragraph{Large $A$ (closes $A > 0.14$).} The classical sextic dominates:
$F_{\rm cl} \sim (v/6) N_6 A^6$ with $C_6 = (v/6)N_6 = 22809.6 > 0$,
while $\tfrac12\Delta I = O(\ln A)$, $C_{\rm unif} = O(A^2)$, and the
second-order off-diagonal share is bounded by
\[
|\Delta F^{(2)}_{\rm offdiag}| \le \tfrac14 \sum_{Q\neq0} W(Q)^2\, B(0),
\qquad B(0) = -M'(\hat r) \sim \tfrac{q_0^2}{8\pi\sqrt{c}}\,\hat r^{-3/2},
\quad \hat r \sim 5vN_4A^4 ,
\]
so $W^2 B \sim A^8 \cdot A^{-6} = O(A^2) \ll A^6$. Scripted spot check:
$\Delta F_{\rm est}(0.30) = +15.9 > \tfrac12 C_6 (0.30)^6$. The scan
window $[0.02, 0.14]$ together with these two bounds covers all $A \ge 0$.

excluded by the alternating-sign structure of the expansion
