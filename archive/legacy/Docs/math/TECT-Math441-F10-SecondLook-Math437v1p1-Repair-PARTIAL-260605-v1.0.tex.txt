\subsubsection*{(a) Lemma 3 --- CONFIRMED (re-derived from scratch)}

\paragraph{(a-1) The ln-inequality and its direction.}  For $x > 0$,
$\ln x \ge 1 - 1/x$ with equality iff $x = 1$ (concavity of $\ln$ at
$x = 1$).  With $x = (m + D)/(r_R + D) > 0$ for all $m > 0$,
$D = C(q^2 - q_0^2)^2 \ge 0$:
\[
\ln\frac{m+D}{r_R+D} \;\ge\; 1 - \frac{r_R+D}{m+D}
\;=\; \frac{m - r_R}{m+D}
\quad\text{pointwise in } q,\ \text{for ALL } m > 0
\]
--- the inequality is sign-correct for $m < r_R$ as well (both sides
negative there; the bound is unconditional).  Integrating against
$q^2\,dq/(2\pi^2)$:
\[
dI(m, r_R) \;\ge\; (m - r_R)\,M(m). \tag{$\ast$}
\]
\emph{Scoping check (dispatch (a)(i))}: the v1.1 statement scopes the
floor to $m \ge r_R$ ($M \le M_R$).  The inequality itself holds for
all $m > 0$; the scoping governs only where $P_B > 0$ is useful.
Verified application map: Intervals B, II, II$'$ all have
$M < M_+ = 0.05107 < M_R = 0.10941$, i.e.\ $m > m_+ > r_R$ --- ALL
$P_B$ applications are inside the stated scope, and \textbf{no
interval with $M > M_R$ relies on $P_B$} (Interval I$'$ uses Lemma-1
all-positivity plus $\Delta F_0 \ge 0$, not $P_B$).  Consistent.

\paragraph{(a-2) The cancellation algebra (dispatch (a)(ii)).}
Substituting $(\ast)$ into
$\Delta F_0(m) = \tfrac12 dI + \tfrac12[(r-m)M - (r-r_R)M_R]
+ \tfrac34 u(M^2 - M_R^2) + \tfrac52 v(M^3 - M_R^3)$:
\[
\tfrac12(m - r_R)M + \tfrac12(r - m)M \;=\; \tfrac12 M\,(r - r_R)
\]

\[
