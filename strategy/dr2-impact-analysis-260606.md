# DR-2 impact analysis: what an unconditional sphere additive-energy bound would subsume

**Kind**: strategy / dependency analysis (non-tier-bearing, governance/development-history.md §7).
**Bears on**: B5-BEYOND-LAYER-BOUND, B1-RH-ENUM, B2-PROPA-HLAYER, gate STEP-5B, results R-001..R-011.
**First issued**: 2026-06-06.

## Question

If DR-2 (the sphere structure dichotomy → the sharp unconditional bound
`Σ_{t≠0} w_t² ≤ K(λ'I)²` for ALL patterns, closing STEP-5B over the
*unrestricted* class) were proven, how much of the existing Sector-B chain
becomes unnecessary?

## Short answer

DR-2 would make the **physical workaround (the H-ADM-COH admissibility ladder)
and the conditional/provisional closure routes** unnecessary, but it would
**not** retire the mathematical foundations — and several of those foundations
are precisely the parts DR-2 is *built from*. It is a strengthening
(unconditional → drops one hypothesis), not a replacement of the core.

## Subsumed (would leave the load-bearing chain)

The whole point of H-ADM-COH was to close STEP-5B by *restricting the class*,
deliberately sidestepping the open circle-incidence conjecture that DR-2 sits
next to. With an unconditional bound, the restriction is no longer needed:

- **H-ADM-COH** (the admissibility amendment) and the **AddB / AddC / AddD /
  AddE** ladder built to make it energy-faithful (coherence cutoff R-009,
  indistinguishability lemma R-005, cross-recombination pin, endpoint
  hardening, the adoption record) → demoted to historical / alternative route.
- **H-KBAL amplitude-dyadic lift** (R-008) → subsumed (DR-2 is all-amplitude by
  construction).
- **20/9 provisional incidence route** (R-006) → superseded by DR-2 (its sharp
  form).
- **Conditional `N_max(I)` closed region and the `c_R = 4√14 · √n` route** →
  weak special cases of the unconditional `O(N²)` bound.

## Survives (DR-2 does not touch it)

1. **P²-representation theorem / structural floor (R-001).** DR-2 bounds the ℓ²
   mass `Σw²` (the numerator of the envelope); R-001 supplies the `(1−a₀)`
   denominator and the operator-positivity `D+W ⪰ D₀`. Both are needed to turn
   an additive-energy bound into a free-energy statement.
2. **Layer margin / Proposition A (B2).** The diagonal/layer comparison and its
   margin `m` are independent of the beyond-layer additive energy. Untouched
   (with H-LAYER, H-ANCHOR).
3. **Near-gap structural floor (R-U10-3 / R-010).** The small-amplitude corner
   of the selection rests on the floor, not on `Σw²`. Still needed.
4. **SC-SCOPE.** DR-2 is a *second-cumulant* additive-energy bound; it does not
   lift the third-cumulant endpoint marginality. SC-SCOPE remains a hypothesis.
5. **Single-circle theorem (R-002), antipodal-carrier partition (R-003),
   ν*=μ_C identity (R-004).** These are the **building blocks DR-2 is
   constructed from** (the dichotomy is stated over the carrier partition and
   uses the single-circle bound). Far from unnecessary, they become *more*
   central.

## Tier consequences if DR-2 lands

- STEP-5B: CLOSED-CONDITIONAL → CLOSED (unconditional within second-cumulant
  scope).
- B5: T5 PINNED-CLOSURE → candidate T6 (the conditional closure becomes a
  theorem; the chain's T4-grade admissibility lemmas drop out).
- B1: drops **H-ADM-COH** from its hypothesis set → T6 on {H-LAYER, H-ANCHOR,
  SC-SCOPE} (4 → 3). Still conditional on the layer hypotheses and SC-SCOPE, so
  **not a clean T7**.

## Strategic read

DR-2 is **research-grade** — adjacent to the unit-circle point-circle incidence
conjecture, a known-hard open problem; the elementary routes all converge on
the same `√n` wall. The admissibility route was chosen *specifically to avoid*
this and it already delivered a (conditional) closure. So:

- The existing work is **not wasted** even if DR-2 is later proven: it closed
  STEP-5B *now*, its foundations (R-001..R-004, R-007) are DR-2's own
  ingredients, and the development is traceable.
- DR-2 is a **high-risk / high-reward strengthening** (removes one hypothesis
  and the admissibility scaffolding), not a prerequisite.

**Recommendation**: treat DR-2 as an optional unconditional-upgrade track, not
the critical path. Higher-certainty next steps that *also* survive a future
DR-2 are: write out G-A0-DUI (textbook), the SC-SCOPE all-orders lift, and
ESTIMATOR-UPGRADE — none of which DR-2 would render unnecessary.
