\section*{1. Purpose and scope}

The whole Sector-B analysis is pinned to the production anchor
$\mu^2 = 0.005$; ROBUSTNESS-MU2 asks whether the Reading-H selection
survives in a $\mu^2$-neighbourhood. This note advances the gate by
settling its A=0-uniqueness component and bounding the $\mu^2$-variation
of the constants the remaining components depend on. It does NOT close
the gate: the exact off-anchor re-margining of the STEP-5B closure is
registered as the narrowed residual.

\section*{2. Structural $\mu^2$-independence of the sign decomposition}

The lemmas behind the A=0 uniqueness (note ga0-dui-closure) use only:
$u < 0$, $v > 0$, $M' < 0$ (L1, $\mu^2$-independent), $u_{\rm eff}(M_c) =
u + 10vM_c = 0$ with $M_c = -u/(10v)$ ($\mu^2$-independent), and the
closed-form bound of L3. NONE references the specific value of $\mu^2$.
Hence the sign decomposition holds for every $\mu^2 > 0$ by structure;
only the two anchor inequalities $m^* > m_w$ and $M_R > M_c$ carry the
anchor value.

\section*{3. The $\mu^2$-cancellation and the robustness radius}

\begin{proposition}[$\mu^2$ cancels in the margin]
$m^* - m_w = 3u\,M_R + 15v\,(M_R^2 - M_c^2)$, independent of $\mu^2$
except through $M_R(\mu^2)$.
\end{proposition}
\begin{proof}
$m^* = \mu^2 + 3uM_R + 15vM_R^2$ (gap equation) and $m_w = \mu^2 +
15vM_c^2$; subtracting removes $\mu^2$. (Verified to $10^{-6}$ at
$\mu^2 = 0.0013, 0.005, 0.02$.)
\end{proof}

\noindent Since $M_R(\mu^2)$ is monotone and varies by $< 2\%$ across the
$\times4$ band ($0.1097 \to 0.1084$), the margin $m^* - m_w$ is nearly
flat. Numerically (sweep $\mu^2 \in [0.00125, 0.02]$, $\times0.25$ to
$\times4$): $m^*/m_w$ ranges $8.37 \to 5.74$ and $M_R/M_c$ ranges
$4.13 \to 4.08$ -- both comfortably above $1$ throughout. Extending the
fine sweep, $m^*/m_w > 2$ and $M_R/M_c > 1.5$ hold on
$\mu^2 \in [0.001, 0.05] = [\times0.2, \times10]$ of the anchor. The
A=0 uniqueness is therefore robust on an order-of-magnitude neighbourhood.

\section*{4. Constants for the remaining components}

The layer margin (Prop A) and the STEP-5B closure margins depend on
$\mu^2$ through the dressed constants $\lambda'(\mu^2) = 3u + 30vM_R$ and
$\hat r(\mu^2)$. Across the $\times4$ band $\lambda'$ decreases smoothly
and monotonically $8.080 \to 7.957$ (a $1.5\%$ variation, no sign change)
and $M_R$ by $< 2\%$. A variation this small cannot collapse the STEP-5B
floors ($\times59.4/\times8.8/\times2.6$ at the anchor) or the layer
margin; however, the EXACT off-anchor margins are not recomputed here
(the full beyond-layer machinery is one anchor-intensity per run). This
is the registered residual: \emph{re-margin the STEP-5B closure on the
$\mu^2$-neighbourhood}.
