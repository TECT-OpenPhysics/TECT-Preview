\section*{2. Step 1 -- linearise the 2PI gap equation}

The gap equation $G^{-1}=K_0+\lambda'\mathcal P^2+\Sigma_2[G]$ linearised around the
common $G_*^{\rm common}(I)$ gives, with $\delta(G^{-1})=-G^{-1}\delta G\,G^{-1}$,
\[
\delta G_*^{\rm pd}
= -\big(1 + G_* K_{\rm BS} G_*\big)^{-1}\,G_*\,\underbrace{\lambda'(\mathcal P^2-\langle\mathcal P^2\rangle)}_{\text{source}=O(a_0)}\,G_*,
\qquad K_{\rm BS}=\frac{\delta\Sigma_2}{\delta G},
\]
where $K_{\rm BS}$ is the Bethe--Salpeter (2PI) kernel and the source is the
pattern-specific self-energy, $O(a_0)\hat r$.

\section*{3. Step 2 -- the response is SCREENED (no strong-coupling amplification)}

The response operator $(1+G_* K_{\rm BS}G_*)^{-1}$ is where a strong coupling could
blow up. For the REPULSIVE density channel ($\lambda'=3u_{\rm eff}>0$) the kernel is
positive, $\|G_* K_{\rm BS} G_*\| = g = \lambda' B_d = 1.03$, so
\[
\boxed{\;\big\|(1+G_* K_{\rm BS}G_*)^{-1}\big\| = \frac{1}{1+g}=0.49\quad(\text{SCREENED, not } \tfrac{1}{1-g}).\;}
\]
The strong coupling $g>1$ does NOT amplify $\delta G_*$: the sign is $1+g$, not
$1-g$ -- the SAME repulsive screening that made the susceptibility finite. Hence
\[
\|\delta G_*^{\rm pd}\| \le \frac{1}{1+g}\,a_0\,\|G_*\| = 0.49\,a_0\,\|G_*\|
= O(a_0),
\]
ESTABLISHED including the self-consistent feedback. This resolves the ordered-BCC
amplification worry: at strong coupling the feedback is screened, not divergent.

\section*{4. Step 3 -- the skeleton sensitivity (the residual)}

\[
|\Delta\Gamma_2^{\rm pd}|
\le \|\Sigma_2^{\rm pd}\|\,\|\delta G_*^{\rm pd}\|
+ \tfrac12\|K_{\rm BS}\|\,\|\delta G_*^{\rm pd}\|^2,
\]
with the second-order term $\tfrac12 g (0.49 a_0)^2 = 0.001$ negligible. The linear
term dominates: $c = \dfrac{\|\Sigma_2^{\rm pd}\|}{\Delta F_{\rm margin}}\times0.49$,
where $\|\Sigma_2^{\rm pd}\|$ is the pattern-dependent skeleton self-energy -- the
SC-SCOPE SUNSET scale (the $n=3$ datum, $\sim4\%$). Thus $c\sim0.02$ and
$c\,a_0\sim0.002\ll1$ plausibly. The rigorous $\|\Sigma_2^{\rm pd}\|$ (the
SC-SCOPE sunset self-energy, already computed at the difference level) is the sole
remaining quantity.
