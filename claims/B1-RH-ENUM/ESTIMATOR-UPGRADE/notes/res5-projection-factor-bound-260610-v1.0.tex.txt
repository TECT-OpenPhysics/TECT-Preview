\section*{1. Purpose and scope}

res5-endpoint-2pi-bound v1.1 carried two closure levers for the $I=2\times10^{-3}$
endpoint: the DR-2 floor $\kappa$, and the pattern projection
$\chi_{\rm proj}\le0.82$. This note tests the projection lever by directly computing
the screened response at the pattern-modulation transfers. The result is a HONEST
NEGATIVE: $\chi_{\rm proj}=1.25>1$, and the operator-norm tail estimate was an
under-estimate. Scope: the $I=2\times10^{-3}$ endpoint; the BCC $\{110\}$ modulation.
No tier action.

\section*{2. The bubble is forward-peaked; screening is maximal at forward}

The a0-skeleton response factor is $C_G=1/(1+g)$, $g=\lambda'\chi_0(0)=1.03$, where
$\chi_0(k)=\int\frac{d^3p}{(2\pi)^3}G(p)G(|p+k|)$ is the bubble and
$\chi_0(0)=\int q^2G^2/2\pi^2=B_d$. Direct evaluation gives $\chi_0(0)=0.128$ and
$\chi_0(k)$ MONOTONE DECREASING in $|k|$ (forward-peaked). Hence the screening
factor
\[
f(k)=\frac{1}{1+\lambda'\chi_0(k)}\quad\text{is MINIMAL at }k=0\ (f(0)=C_G=0.49)\ \text{and INCREASES with }|k|.
\]
The screening $C_G$ is the MAXIMAL screening (forward channel), not a generic bound.

\section*{3. The {110} modulation lives where screening is weaker}

The modulation $\lambda'(P^2-\langle P^2\rangle)$ is supported on the BCC $\{110\}$
transfers $|t|/q_0\in\{1,\sqrt2,\sqrt3,2\}$, with weights $w_t^2$ (the same additive-
energy structure as the floor). Every transfer is away from forward, so
\[
f(t)\in\{0.567,0.626,0.679,0.728\}\ >\ C_G=0.492,\qquad \frac{f(t)}{C_G}\in[1.15,1.48].
\]
The $w_t^2$-weighted average is $f_{\rm avg}=0.613$, hence
\[
\boxed{\ \chi_{\rm proj}=\frac{f_{\rm avg}}{C_G}=\frac{0.613}{0.492}=1.25\ >\ 1\ \ (\text{not }<0.82).\ }
\]

\section*{4. Verdict: projection route eliminated; tail corrected; solely DR-2-gated}

\noindent\textbf{Projection lever REFUTED.} $\chi_{\rm proj}=1.25>0.82$: the
res5-endpoint v1.1 projection lever is ELIMINATED. The modulation does not live in
the maximally-screened channel, so the screening does not help beyond $C_G$ -- it
helps LESS.

\noindent\textbf{Tail CORRECTION.} The operator-norm/screening estimate
$C_{\rm higher}\sim C_G a_0=0.047$ assumed forward screening; the corrected value is
\[
C_{\rm higher}=f_{\rm avg}\,a_0=0.059\ >\ 0.047.
\]
This corrects the a0-skeleton's $C_G a_0$ upward by the factor $\chi_{\rm proj}=1.25$.

\noindent\textbf{Endpoint now solely DR-2-gated.} The corrected tail is still
bracketed, $\text{slack}_{\rm proved}(0.0385)<0.059<\text{slack}_{\rm verified}
(0.0758)$, so the endpoint closes ONLY at the verified floor, now with a $23\%$
margin (was $38\%$ under the under-estimate), and is OPEN at the conservative floor.
The endpoint rests SOLELY on the DR-2 floor $\kappa$ route; the second lever is gone.
Off-endpoint ($I\le10^{-3}$) closure is UNAFFECTED -- the $\ge27\times$ margin absorbs
the $1.25$ factor (e.g. $I=10^{-3}$: tail $1.25\times0.025=0.031\ll$ slack $0.68$).
No tier flip: B1-RH-ENUM stays T6 CONDITIONAL on $\{$H-LAYER$\}$.
