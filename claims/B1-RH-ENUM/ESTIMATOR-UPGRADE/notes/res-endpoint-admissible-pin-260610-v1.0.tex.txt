\section*{1. Purpose and scope}

The RES-5 endpoint (res5-dr2-kappa-bound v1.2) and H-diag (hdiag-offdiag-floor-bound)
both reduce to a bound on $K_{\rm floor}=E_+/N^2-1$ of the competitor. v1.2 left the
class-wide constant sufficiency ASSERTED (T-014) and the non-uniform amplitude case a
separate residual (T-015). This note pins both numerically over the admissible
crystallographic-shell class. Scope: $N\le n_{\rm pack}=41$; crystallographic-shell
unions; the chi$(P)\lesssim T'$ link is the standing operator-decision residual. No
tier action.

\section*{2. The two thresholds}

\[
\text{RES-5 endpoint: }K_{\rm floor}<26.2,
\qquad
\text{H-diag/RES-1: }K_{\rm floor}<20.5,
\qquad K_{\rm floor}=\frac{E_+(Q)}{N^2}-1.
\]
The tighter (binding) threshold is the RES-1 one, $20.5$.

\section*{3. T-014: uniform pin}

The EXACT worst $K_{\rm floor}$ over the admissible class -- single shells, cumulative
lattice balls $\{|x|^2\le R\}$, and all unions of up to four small shells, with $N\le
41$ (130 configurations) -- is
\[
\boxed{\ \max_{Q}K_{\rm floor}^{\rm unif}=12.13\quad(\text{union }R\in\{1,3,5\},\ N=38).\ }
\]
This is below $20.5$ (factor $1.69$) and $26.2$ (factor $2.16$). The worst case is a
dense small-radius shell union -- additive energy is maximised by small co-circular
shells; larger-radius or sparser configurations are strictly easier. The
``$C_\varepsilon R^\varepsilon<26.2$'' assertion of v1.2 is thus an EXACT numerical pin.

\section*{4. T-015: non-uniform amplitudes via R-027}

For arbitrary amplitudes $A$, the weighted Lemma A (R-027) gives $\sum_{t\ne0}w_t^2\le
T'(Q)\,\|A\|_2^4$, hence
\[
K_{\rm floor}(A)=\frac{\sum_{t\ne0}w_t^2}{I^2}\le T'(Q)
\qquad(\text{any amplitudes}).
\]
The EXACT worst sum-circle richness over the admissible class is $\max_Q T'=13$, below
$20.5$ (factor $1.58$) and $26.2$ (factor $2.02$). So NON-UNIFORM amplitude competitors
also close, for both the RES-5 endpoint and H-diag.

\[
\boxed{\ K_{\rm floor}\le12.13\ (\text{uniform}),\quad \le T'=13\ (\text{non-uniform}),\quad
\text{both}<20.5<26.2.\ }

The RES-5 endpoint AND H-diag/RES-1 close for both uniform and non-uniform amplitude
