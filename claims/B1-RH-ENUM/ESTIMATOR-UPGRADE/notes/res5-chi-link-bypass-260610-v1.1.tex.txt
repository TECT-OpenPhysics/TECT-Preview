\section*{2. The weighted Lemma A is the chi(P)-free link (with proof sketch)}

The STEP-5B floor requires $\sum_t w_t^2\le K(\lambda'I)^2$ with $w_t=\sum_{u+v=t}c_uc_v$.
Write $r(t)=\#\{(u,v)\in M\times M:u+v=t\}$ for the pair multiplicity of the transfer
$t$. By Cauchy--Schwarz on the $r(t)$ admissible pairs,
\[
w_t^2=\Bigl(\sum_{u+v=t}c_uc_v\Bigr)^2\ \le\ r(t)\sum_{u+v=t}|c_u|^2|c_v|^2 .
\]
For $t\ne0$ the pairs $(u,v)$ with $u+v=t$ have both summands on the sum-level circle
$C_t=\{x:x\cdot t=|t|^2/2\}$, so $r(t)\le\#(M\cap C_t)\le T'(Q)=\max_{t\ne0}\#(M\cap C_t)$.
Summing and using $\sum_t\sum_{u+v=t}|c_u|^2|c_v|^2=\bigl(\sum_u|c_u|^2\bigr)^2=\|c\|_2^4$,
\[
\sum_{t\ne0}w_t^2\ \le\ T'(Q)\sum_{t\ne0}\sum_{u+v=t}|c_u|^2|c_v|^2\ \le\ T'(Q)\,\|c\|_2^4 ,
\]
which is the $t\ne0$ part of the WEIGHTED Lemma A $\sum_t w_t^2\le(1+T'(Q))\|c\|_2^4$
(R-027, dr2-step5b-integration v1.1, T7).\footnote{The $t=0$ channel is split off
separately: for a real field with the antipodal pairing $c_{-u}=\overline{c_u}$ and the
normalisation $\|c\|_2^2=I$, $w_0=\sum_{u}c_u c_{-u}=\sum_u|c_u|^2=\|c\|_2^2=I$ exactly,
contributing the ``$1$'' in $(1+T')$; the floor uses only the $t\ne0$ part.} Since
$w_0=I$,
\[
\boxed{\ K_{\rm floor}(c)=\frac{\sum_{t\ne0}w_t^2}{I^2}\ \le\ T'(Q)\qquad\text{for ANY amplitudes.}\ }
\]
The additive-energy $\to$ STEP-5B-floor connection is THIS weighted bridge. The
carrier-richness $\chi(P)$ is the DUAL extraction-route incidence quantity; the
integration route uses $T'$ directly and does NOT invoke $\chi(P)$. (Verified: over a
random-amplitude subset of 115 configs $\times$ 3 random amplitude profiles,
