\section*{2. The layer margin is closed-form in mu-squared}

The Prop-A layer margin (Math437 v1.2 / Math440) is the band penalty floor minus
the band dip,
\[
\mathrm{MARGIN}(\mu^2) = P_B\!\bigl(M_+(\mu^2)\bigr) - \mathrm{DIP}_{\rm BAND}(\mu^2),
\]
with (production convention, bare parameter $R = \mu^2$, gap point
$r_R(\mu^2)$, $M_R(\mu^2) = M(r_R)$):
\[
M_+(\mu^2) = \frac{-3u + \sqrt{9u^2 - 60 v\,\mu^2}}{30 v},
\qquad
\hat r_0(M_c) = \mu^2 - \frac{3u^2}{20 v},
\qquad
\mathrm{DIP}_{\rm BAND} = \frac{|\hat r_0(M_c)|^{3/2}}{3\sqrt{v}},
\]
\[
P_B(M) = \tfrac12(\mu^2 - r_R)(M - M_R) + \tfrac34 u\,(M^2 - M_R^2)
         + \tfrac52 v\,(M^3 - M_R^3).
\]
At the anchor this reproduces the certified value exactly:
$M_+ = 0.0510720$, $P_B(M_+) = 0.0052460$, $\mathrm{DIP}_{\rm BAND} =
0.0009260$, so $\mathrm{MARGIN}(0.005) = 0.0043200$, validating the closed form
against the frozen constant $0.00432$.
