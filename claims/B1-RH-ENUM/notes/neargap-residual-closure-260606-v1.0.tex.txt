\[
\delta_{\rm 2nd} \;=\; 6.7\times10^{-8}
\qquad\Bigl(\text{relative } \frac{6.7\times10^{-8}}{1.61\times10^{-3}}
= 4.2\times10^{-5}\Bigr).
\]
The discrepancy is the quadratic Taylor remainder of the
self-consistent map $I \mapsto \hat r(I)$, hence scales as
$(I/I_{\rm cal})^2$:
\[
\delta_{\rm 2nd}(I = 2\times10^{-3}) \;\le\; 6.7\times10^{-8}
\times 20^2 \;=\; 2.7\times10^{-5}
\;\ll\; \underbrace{\sim\!2\lambda' M_R\, I = 3.5\times10^{-3}}_{
\text{lemma's linear protection at } 2\times10^{-3}} ,
\]
a $\times130$ margin floor at the worst certified intensity (and
$\times1300$ at the production anchor, where the remainder is

self-consistent map's curvature is built from $u, v$, and the

intensity $I$ (the pattern enters $\hat r$ through $I$ alone in the
convention, and the response functions are isotropic integrals); the
LAM-point calibration therefore bounds the remainder for EVERY pattern

\begin{itemize}
\item[($\alpha$)] \emph{``The $(I/I_{\rm cal})^2$ scaling of the
remainder assumes the self-consistent map is analytic with the

could spoil it.''} VALID-with-mitigation: the map's expansion
parameter is $2\lambda' I/\hat r \le 0.096$ at the endpoint, so the
cubic term is suppressed by another factor $\le 0.1$ relative to the
quadratic --- folding it in changes the $\times130$ floor by at most
$10\%$. The R-U10-3 script can verify the remainder directly at the
endpoint (one extra self-consistent solve) at negligible cost; added
to its registered spec.
\item[($\beta$)] \emph{``Class representativity leans on `the pattern
enters only through $I$' --- but $\lambda'$ itself contains $M(\hat
r)$ and different patterns at the same $I$ have identical $\hat r$
only BY the convention; the argument is circular.''} DISMISSED with
the convention's content: within the matched bookkeeping the
isotropic dressing IS defined by the $(I, \hat r)$ self-consistency
--- that is hypothesis H-LAYER's layer framing, already a named
hypothesis of the U1 set; GIVEN the layer, the dressing of two
patterns with equal $I$ coincides by construction, and the remainder
bound is class-uniform. The objection re-discovers H-LAYER, which is
tracked, not hidden.
\item[($\gamma$)] \emph{``Section 3 asserts the diagonal gain is
booked inside $\Delta F_0$ without exhibiting the chain's $F_0$
definition line-by-line.''} VALID-with-mitigation: the assertion is
the natural reading (the layer's $F_0(m)$ is the isotropic-Gaussian
free energy whose fluctuation part is $\tfrac1{2V}\operatorname{tr}
\ln(m + C(\cdot)^2)$, Math437's slaved family), and the rearrangement
identity is independent of WHERE the chain books the diagonal gain
(any booking is one of the partitions). The line-by-line exhibit
against the Math424-AddA production decomposition folds into the

identity's validity does not wait on it.
\end{itemize}
