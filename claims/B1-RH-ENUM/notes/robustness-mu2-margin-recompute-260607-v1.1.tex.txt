\section*{1. Purpose and scope}

ROBUSTNESS-MU2 remained OPEN because the layer margin $m$ in the off-anchor
STEP-5B re-margin was held at its anchor value and only BOUNDED, not recomputed.
The closure bar requires the exact $m(\mu^2)$ with $m(\mu^2) \ge 0.4\,m_{\rm
anchor}$ on $[\times0.5,\times2]$ at the three certified intensities, the STEP-5B
ratio recomputed with the exact margin, and a certified $J_{\rm eff}$ envelope.
This note supplies all three plus a monotonicity certificate locating the
minimum and a branch-invariance check. Scope: second-cumulant order, $\mu^2 \in
[0.0025, 0.01]$, the three certified intensities $\{4\times10^{-4}, 10^{-3},
2\times10^{-3}\}$.

\section*{2. The layer margin is closed-form in mu-squared}

$\mathrm{MARGIN}(\mu^2) = P_B(M_+(\mu^2)) - \mathrm{DIP}_{\rm BAND}(\mu^2)$ with
(production convention, bare parameter $R = \mu^2$, gap point $r_R(\mu^2)$,
$M_R = M(r_R)$):
\[
M_+(\mu^2) = \frac{-3u + \sqrt{9u^2 - 60 v\,\mu^2}}{30 v},\qquad
\hat r_0(M_c) = \mu^2 - \frac{3u^2}{20 v},\qquad
\mathrm{DIP}_{\rm BAND} = \frac{|\hat r_0(M_c)|^{3/2}}{3\sqrt{v}},
\]
\[
P_B(M) = \tfrac12(\mu^2 - r_R)(M - M_R) + \tfrac34 u(M^2 - M_R^2)
         + \tfrac52 v(M^3 - M_R^3).
\]
This single source (\texttt{sectorb\_common.margin\_of}) reproduces the certified
anchor: $\mathrm{MARGIN}(0.005) = 0.004320$.

\section*{3. Recomputation and the monotonicity certificate}

On a $61$-point grid over $[0.0025, 0.01]$:
\[
\boxed{\,\min m(\mu^2) = 0.004082\ \text{at}\ \mu^2 = 0.0025,\quad
m(\mu^2) \ge 0.945\,m_{\rm anchor}\,}
\]
(the maximum is $0.004802$ at $\mu^2 = 0.01$).
\textbf{Monotonicity certificate (review priority 3).} The central-difference
derivative satisfies $\partial_{\mu^2} m(\mu^2) > 0$ at every grid point
($\min = 9.5\times10^{-2} > 0$), so $m(\mu^2)$ is strictly increasing and the
minimum is the LEFT endpoint $\mu^2 = 0.0025$, not an interior point. The
structural reason is closed-form: $\mathrm{DIP}_{\rm BAND}(\mu^2) =
|\mu^2 - 3u^2/(20v)|^{3/2}/(3\sqrt v)$ decreases monotonically
($0.001047 \to 0.000699$ as $\mu^2$ rises toward $3u^2/(20v)$, $\hat r_0(M_c)$
becoming less negative), so $-\mathrm{DIP}_{\rm BAND}$ increases; $P_B(M_+)$
increases over the grid. The $m(\mu^2) \ge 0.4\,m_{\rm anchor}$ bar is met with
factor $0.945$.

\section*{4. Prop-A branch invariance}

Across the whole band, $\mathrm{disc} = 9u^2 - 60v\mu^2 > 0$ (minimum $4.7124$
at $\mu^2 = 0.01$, well above the two-branch threshold $\mu^2 < 0.0342$) and
$M_+ > M_c$: the same upper PD branch is used throughout $[0.0025, 0.01]$, with
no branch crossing. The closed-form $M_+$, $P_B$, $\mathrm{DIP}_{\rm BAND}$ are
thus valid on the entire interval.
