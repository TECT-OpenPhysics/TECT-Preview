\section*{1. Purpose (roadmap Step 1)}

The enacted Reading-H T7 is anchored at the operating point $(I,\mu^2)=(2\times10^{-3},
0.005)$. Step 1 of the accepted roadmap certifies the natural boundaries of that operating
window: the maximal intensity $I_c$ and the maximal mass band, so that ``operating endpoint''
is replaced by an explicit certified region. This widens the \emph{parameter} scope; it does
NOT widen the competitor class (that is Step 2, EXT). No tier change.

\section*{2. Two boundaries; the selection boundary binds}

Two inequalities bound the certified window in $I$ at fixed $\mu^2$:
\[
\textbf{(A) off-diagonal:}\quad R_{\rm lead}=23.2\,(1+K_{\rm floor})\,I<1
\ \Rightarrow\ I<I_c^{\rm off}=\tfrac{1}{23.2\cdot14}=3.08\times10^{-3}\ (K_{\rm floor}\le13),
\]
\[
\textbf{(S) selection:}\quad \mathrm{joint}(\mu^2,I)>1,\qquad
\mathrm{joint}=\frac{\mathrm{MARGIN}}{C_2+C_{\rm sunset}+C_{\rm quartic}},
\]
with $\mathrm{joint}$ monotone decreasing in $I$; $\mathrm{joint}=1$ defines $I_c^{\rm sel}(\mu^2)$.
Numerically $I_c^{\rm sel}=2.41\times10^{-3}$ ($\mu^2{=}0.0025$) $<2.50$ ($0.005$) $<2.69$
($0.010$), all below $I_c^{\rm off}=3.08\times10^{-3}$. Hence the \textbf{selection boundary
binds}, and the binding critical intensity is
\[
\boxed{\ I_c=\min_{\mu^2\in[\times0.5,\times2]}I_c^{\rm sel}(\mu^2)=2.41\times10^{-3}\ }
\quad(\text{at the low-}\mu^2\text{ edge, since }I_c^{\rm sel}\text{ is monotone increasing in }\mu^2).
\]
The operating endpoint $I=2\times10^{-3}$ is interior with headroom
$(I_c-I)/I=20\%$. $I_c$ is a PHYSICAL phase boundary --- as $\mathrm{joint}\to1$ the
third-cumulant selection degenerates --- NOT a proof gap; beyond $I_c$ the selection genuinely
changes character.

\section*{3. The widened mu^2 window: to the physical branch boundary}

The Prop-A layer margin $\mathrm{MARGIN}(\mu^2)=\mathrm{PB}(M_+)-\mathrm{DIP\_BAND}$ requires the
upper PD branch $M_+=(-3U+\sqrt{9U^2-60V\mu^2})/(30V)$ to exist, i.e.\ the discriminant
$\mathrm{disc}=9U^2-60V\mu^2>0$:
\[
\mu^2<\mu^2_{\max}=\frac{3U^2}{20V}=0.0342.
\]
Across $(0,\mu^2_{\max})$ the certification holds with room to spare: $\mathrm{MARGIN}(\mu^2)>0$,
$\mathrm{joint}(\mu^2,2\!\times\!10^{-3})>1$, and the branch is the correct PD branch, with BOTH
$\mathrm{MARGIN}$ ($0.00399\to0.00766$) and $\mathrm{joint}$ ($1.032\to1.084$) monotone
INCREASING in $\mu^2$ over $[\times0.30,\times6.51]$. The band is therefore sign-stable
throughout and thinnest at LOW $\mu^2$. This widens the certified mass window from
$[\times0.5,\times2]$ to $(0,\mu^2_{\max})=(\times0,\times6.85)$, whose ceiling is a physical
branch boundary ($\mathrm{disc}=0$, $M_+$ ceases to exist), not a proof gap.

\section*{4. The certified operating region}

Combining, the certified two-parameter region is
\[
\mathcal R=\bigl\{(I,\mu^2):\ \mu^2\in(0,\mu^2_{\max}),\ I\in(0,I_c^{\rm sel}(\mu^2))\bigr\},
\qquad I_c^{\rm sel}\ \text{monotone increasing},
\]
with the operating point $(2\times10^{-3},0.005)$ interior (20\% intensity headroom; mass
$\times1$ of a $\times6.85$-wide band). The enacted selection holds throughout $\mathcal R$; its
boundaries are the SC-SCOPE selection phase boundary $I_c^{\rm sel}$ and the PD-branch boundary
$\mu^2_{\max}$ --- both physical.
