\begin{itemize}
\item[($\alpha$)] \emph{``Same-ecosystem re-execution is not independent
verification.''} VALID with mitigation: the asserts are the original
authors' self-tests; reproducibility, not correctness, is certified here.
Independent correctness evidence is the migrated Math434 \S 15.5
independent-audit note; CI re-execution on the GitHub runner adds an
environment-independent check.
\item[($\beta$)] \emph{``Math434/Math436 completed in a single budget window,
so the checkpoint-resume path went unexercised in this re-validation.''}
VALID: recorded as a limitation. The resume path is evidenced by the archived
\url{state.json} provenance files; it is falsifiable at any time by running
under a tighter budget and observing a correct resume.
\item[($\gamma$)] \emph{``Enumerated coverage does not establish
admissible-class exhaustiveness.''} DISMISSED as an objection to this record:
the record claims migration and reproducibility only. Exhaustiveness is
exactly the open gate STEP-5B, explicitly out of scope; \texttt{B1-RH-ENUM}
remains T5 \textsc{closed@estimator-grade}.
\end{itemize}
