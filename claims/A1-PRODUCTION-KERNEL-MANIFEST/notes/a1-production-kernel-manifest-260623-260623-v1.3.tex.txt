\section*{2. The real N-001 backend (v1.3)}

The canonical N-001 solver fixes the kinetic coefficients via
\begin{equation}
(r,Z)=\texttt{kinetic\_coefficients}(\texttt{mu2\_shell},Y,q_0):\qquad
r=\texttt{mu2\_shell}+Y q_0^4,\quad Z=-2Y q_0^2,
\end{equation}
(\texttt{Codes/pde/continuation\_mu2\_v25.py}) and realises the linear operator as
the scalar Bloch symbol
\begin{equation}
D_{\rm backend}(k)=r+Z\,s_2+Y\,s_2^2,\qquad s_2=|k|^2
\end{equation}
(\texttt{bloch\_matrix\_linear} in \texttt{Codes/pde/bloch\_linearization.py},
residual $r\Psi-Z\nabla^2\Psi+Y\nabla^4\Psi$). Both are vendored VERBATIM into
\texttt{actual\_n001\_pde\_backend.py} with the originals pinned by sha256. The
verifier (a) confirms
$\texttt{kinetic\_coefficients}(\texttt{mu2\_shell},Y,q_0)=(\texttt{r\_zero},Z)$
to $10^{-12}$ --- the stored fields ARE the solver's own coefficients --- and (b)
confirms $D_{\rm backend}(k)=\texttt{r\_zero}+Z|k|^2+Y|k|^4$ to $8.9\times10^{-16}$.

\section*{3. Faithfulness, provenance, and teeth}

When the frozen TECT2 solver tree is reachable, the verifier imports the ORIGINAL
\texttt{continuation\_mu2\_v25.py} and asserts the vendored
\texttt{kinetic\_coefficients} reproduces it exactly ($0$ over a random sweep);
otherwise it reports the cross-check SKIPPED and relies on the pinned sha256
(\texttt{continuation} \texttt{6db0393a..}, \texttt{bloch} \texttt{337bd6de..}).
The reader has teeth: corrupting the stored $q_0$ by $1\%$ makes
$\texttt{kinetic\_coefficients}$ diverge from the stored $(r,Z)$ by $O(10^{-2})$.
