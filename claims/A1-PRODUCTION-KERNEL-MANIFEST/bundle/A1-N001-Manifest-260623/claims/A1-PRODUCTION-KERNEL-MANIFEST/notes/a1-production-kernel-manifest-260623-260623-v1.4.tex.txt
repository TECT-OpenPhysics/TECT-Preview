\section*{2. The real N-001 symbol and the eta\_shell=0 slice}

The N-001 solver's linear operator (\texttt{bloch\_matrix\_linear} in
\texttt{bloch\_linearization.py}) has scalar diagonal
\begin{equation}
D_{\rm solver}(k)=r+Z|k|^2+Y|k|^4+\eta_{\rm shell}\,(|k|-q_0)^2,
\qquad (r,Z)=\texttt{kinetic\_coefficients}(\texttt{mu2\_shell},Y,q_0).
\end{equation}
The pure Brazovskii kernel certified by A1/A2/A3 is the $\eta_{\rm shell}=0$ slice.
The config stores $\eta_{\rm shell}=0$ as a sixth physical field; the verifier
ENFORCES $\eta_{\rm shell}=0$ and confirms the ORIGINAL
$\texttt{bloch\_matrix\_linear}(\eta_{\rm shell}=0)$ reduces to
$r_{\rm zero}+Z|k|^2+Y|k|^4=$ the stored kernel ($8.9\times10^{-16}$). The shell-bias
term is load-bearing: setting $\eta_{\rm shell}=0.1$ shifts the ORIGINAL symbol by up
to $0.231$, so $\eta_{\rm shell}=0$ is an essential, non-vacuous condition.
