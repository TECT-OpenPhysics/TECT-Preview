\section*{1. Purpose and scope}

This note records the refutation of \texttt{B3-BCC-STRUCT} and its retirement
per the operator verdict of 2026-06-23. It makes no positive claim. The
refutation evidence (\S2) is accepted; the v1.0 proposal to \emph{reframe} B3
onto B1 is rejected (\S3) and B3 is retired (\S4).

\section*{2. Refutation evidence (accepted)}

The seeded B3 evidence is the single-shell SMA structural ranking (Math194) and
the 1-mode $K_4/K_6$ ranking (Math383). Re-running
\url{archive/legacy/scripts/Math194_brazovskii_lattice_ranking.py} places
\textbf{BCC at rank 9 of 10} (lamellar rank 1, $F/V=-1.211\times10^{-5}$;
bcc $F/V=-4.845\times10^{-6}$) --- the opposite of $F_{\rm BCC}<F_{\rm FCC}<
F_{\rm SC}$. Independently, \textbf{Math400} (2026-05-11, T0 binding) shows that
at $\mu^2=+0.005$ every lattice collapses to the disordered $F=0$ state (the SMA
``BCC minimum'' is a saddle) and refutes the Math383 $K$-table. Registered:
\url{negative-results/registry.md} \texttt{R-2026-06-23-b3-bcc-structural-selection}.

\section*{3. Why the reframe is rejected (operator verdict)}

B1-RH-ENUM establishes the \emph{restricted} proposition
\begin{equation}
\Delta F_{\rm enum}[\mathcal R]:=F_{\rm TECT}[\mathcal R]-F_{\rm TECT}[\mathcal R_H]>0,
\qquad \mathcal R\in\mathcal E_{\rm tested},
\end{equation}
a \emph{relative ranking within the tested ordered-reading ensemble}. It does
\textbf{not} entail
\begin{equation}
F[\mathcal R_H]<F[0]\qquad\text{nor}\qquad H_{\mathcal R_H}\succeq0
\ \text{under unrestricted variations},
\end{equation}
and must not be conflated with the Math400 disordered-collapse / saddle result.
Therefore B1 cannot resurrect the original ``BCC energy-condensate structure is
selected'' claim. Adversarial points upheld by the operator: (i) ``continuum-
anchored'' must mean a continuum-functional \emph{estimator}, not a completed
$a\to0$ PDE continuum limit (PDE continuum certification is open); (ii) keeping
the original card name for a meaning-replaced claim is a meaning substitution,
not a scope reduction; (iii) the reframed ranking falsifier is not a falsifier
of any BCC phase/minimum claim.

\section*{4. Disposition (retirement)}

\texttt{B3-BCC-STRUCT} $\to$ \textbf{T0 REFUTED / RETIRED}. The surviving
