\section*{2. The leading ratio in terms of $K_{\rm floor}$}

The off-diagonal Bogoliubov-Hessian weight is the additive energy
$\sum_Q p_2(Q)^2=E_+$ (proved). The conservative leading condensate-direction ratio is
\[
R_{\rm lead}(I)=\frac{9}{4}\,\frac{u_{\rm eff}^2 B_{\max}E_+}{c_{\rm diag}}\,\frac{I}{N},
\qquad c_{\rm diag}=\tfrac{N}{2}r_R,
\]
and since $c_{\rm diag}\propto N$ and $E_+/N^2=1+K_{\rm floor}$,
\[
\boxed{\ R_{\rm lead}(I)=\frac{9}{2}\,\frac{u_{\rm eff}^2 B_{\max}}{r_R}\,(1+K_{\rm floor})\,I
= \mathrm{const}\cdot(1+K_{\rm floor})\,I,\quad \mathrm{const}=23.22.\ }
\]
$R_{\rm lead}$ is thus controlled by the SAME $K_{\rm floor}=E_+/N^2-1$ as the RES-5
endpoint tail. (BCC: $K_{\rm floor}=2.75\Rightarrow R_{\rm lead}=0.174$, reproducing
the certificate.)
