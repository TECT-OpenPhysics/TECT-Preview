\section*{1. Purpose and scope}

The reduction note (hdiag-offdiag-additive-energy) showed the off-diagonal
Bogoliubov weight equals the additive energy $\sum_Q p_2^2=E_+$ and reduced the
class-wide stability to $R\lesssim u_{\rm eff}^2 B_{\max} r_R^{-1} I(1+T')$,
verifying $R<1$ for BCC (worst $0.916$). The operator's review directed a constant
certificate with four goals: pin $B_{\max}$; a class-wide diagonal lower bound;
bound the $p_4$ / $p_2 p_4$ terms; and assemble the lattice-class ratio. This note
executes all four and reports the honest verdict at the operating intensity.

\section*{2. The four pinned constants}

\paragraph{(1) $B_{\max}$.} The bubble $B(|Q|)=\int_q G_d(q)G_d(q{+}Q)=J(|Q|,\hat r)$
is monotone decreasing in $|Q|$, so $B_{\max}=B(|Q|_{\min})$. For the BCC Bragg set
the smallest nonzero transfer is $|Q|_{\min}=q_0=0.680$, giving
\[
B(0)=0.299,\qquad B_{\max}=B(q_0)=0.218 \le B(0).
\]

\paragraph{(2) Diagonal floor.} Math428 (\S3bis) gives the small-$A$ leading term
$\Delta F_{\rm diag}(A)=c_{\rm diag}A^2+O(A^4)$ with $c_{\rm diag}=n\,r_R=\tfrac{N}{2}r_R=1.827$
($>0$); at finite $A$ the diagonal term only grows, so $\Delta F_{\rm diag}\ge c_{\rm diag}A^2$
is a conservative class-wide lower bound.

\paragraph{(3) $p_4$ / cross terms.} $W(Q)^2 = 9u_{\rm eff}^2A^4 p_2^2
+ 30u_{\rm eff}vA^6 p_2 p_4 + 25v^2A^8 p_4^2$. Relative to the leading $A^4 p_2^2$
term, the cross and quartic carry extra $A^2\sim I/N$ factors. Evaluated at the
operating amplitude,
\[
\frac{30u_{\rm eff}vA^2\sum_Q p_2 p_4}{9u_{\rm eff}^2\sum_Q p_2^2}\Big|_{A^2=I/N}
= 0.044 \ll 1,
\]
so the leading $p_2^2 = E_+$ term controls the bound at the operating intensity.

\paragraph{(4) The assembled ratio.} With $A^2=I/N$ (condensate intensity $I$ over
$N$ modes), $E_+\le(1+T')N^2$, and the worst case $\rho=1$,
\[
R_{\rm lead}(I)\ \le\ \frac{\tfrac14\cdot9\,u_{\rm eff}^2 (I/N)^2 B_{\max}E_+}{c_{\rm diag}(I/N)}
= \frac{9}{4}\,\frac{u_{\rm eff}^2 B_{\max} E_+}{c_{\rm diag}}\,\frac{I}{N}.
\]
