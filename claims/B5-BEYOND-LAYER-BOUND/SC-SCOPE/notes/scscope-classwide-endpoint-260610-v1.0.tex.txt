\section*{2. MARGIN-cancellation: the joint depends only on $\rho_{\rm lat}$}

The certified joint (scscope-quartic-normalisation-certificate v1.0) is
\[
\mathrm{joint}=\frac{\mathrm{MARGIN}}{C_2+C_{\rm sunset}+C_{\rm quartic}},\quad
C_2=\frac{\mathrm{MARGIN}}{\rho_{\rm lat}},\;
C_{\rm sunset}=\frac{\mathrm{MARGIN}(1-1/\rho_{\rm lat})}{S},\;
C_{\rm quartic}=R_{\max}C_2,
\]
in which MARGIN appears in every term and CANCELS:
\[
\boxed{\ \mathrm{joint}(\rho_{\rm lat})
=\frac{1}{\tfrac{1}{\rho_{\rm lat}}(1+R_{\max})+\bigl(1-\tfrac{1}{\rho_{\rm lat}}\bigr)/S}\ }
\]
a monotone increasing function of $\rho_{\rm lat}$, saturating at $S=1.13$ as
$\rho_{\rm lat}\to\infty$ (the sunset cap). Setting $\mathrm{joint}=1$ gives the
critical floor
\[
\rho_{\rm crit}=\frac{1+R_{\max}-1/S}{1-1/S}=4.347 .
\]
Thus the endpoint closes ($\mathrm{joint}>1$) iff $\rho_{\rm lat}>4.347$. (At
$\rho_{\rm lat}=6.55$ this reproduces the certified $\times1.040$.)

\section*{3. The floor is $\rho_{\rm lat}=K_{\rm budget}/(1+T')$; critical $T'=60.4$}

The STEP-5B floor is $\rho_{\rm lat}=K_{\rm budget}/(1+T')$ with
\[
K_{\rm budget}=\frac{4(1-a_0)\,\mathrm{MARGIN}}{(\lambda'I)^2 J_{\rm eff}}=266.7
\quad(\text{anchor}),
\]
PATTERN-INDEPENDENT (it depends on $\mu^2,I$ through the dressed propagator only;
the competitor enters EXCLUSIVELY through its additive-energy richness $T'$, the
sum-circle occupancy). Hence
\[
\mathrm{joint}>1\iff \rho_{\rm lat}>4.347\iff 1+T'<\frac{K_{\rm budget}}{4.347}
\iff \boxed{\ T'<60.4\ }.
\]
