\section*{1. Purpose and scope}

This referee document concerns the STEP-5B rectangle-constant closure: the theorem-grade prefactor $K(n)$ controlling the layer budget. \textbf{Scope:} the rectangle constant and the official threshold. \textbf{Not claimed:} the alternative $20/9$ incidence route is provisional (constant unpinned) and is not relied upon. This package does not by itself close full STEP-5B (the budget comparison and admissibility gates are tracked separately), does not close admissible-class exhaustiveness, and does not enact the full Reading-H $C_{\rm full}$ theorem.

\section*{2. Definitions and the result}

\paragraph{Definitions (patch v1.1-1).} Let $Q \subset S^2_{q_0}$ be an admissible
pattern of $n$ independent modes on the condensation shell, with symmetrised
(antipodal) point set $\hat Q = Q \cup (-Q)$ of $N = 2n$ points and amplitudes
$\{A_a\}$ of total intensity $I = \sum_a A_a^2$. The rectangle prefactor is the
normalised off-diagonal transfer $\ell^2$ mass
\[
K(n) \;=\; \frac{1}{(\lambda' I)^2} \sum_{t \neq 0} w_t^2,
\qquad
w_t \;=\; \lambda' \sum_{\substack{a + b = t \\ a, b \in \hat Q}} A_a A_b ,
\]
i.e.\ the quantity the STEP-5B layer budget must dominate. The balance parameter
$\kappa$ is the registered \textbf{H-KBAL} amplitude-balance constant,
\[
\textbf{H-KBAL}: \quad A_{\max}^2 \;\le\; \kappa^2\, I/n ,
\]
with $\kappa \simeq 1$ at the production operating point (equal-amplitude patterns
have $\kappa = 1$ exactly).

\paragraph{The result.} (i) THEOREM (operator verdicts \#9/\#10): for every
$\kappa$-balanced admissible pattern,
\[
K(n) \;\le\; 8 + 4\sqrt{14}\,\kappa^4 \sqrt{n},
\qquad
c_R^{\rm rigorous} = 4\sqrt{14} \simeq 14.97,
\]
with $1.59\times10^5$ the OFFICIAL threshold (derivation in Sec.~3). (ii) The
$20/9$ incidence route ($2.2\times10^{10}$) is provisional with an unpinned
constant and is recorded but not load-bearing.

\paragraph{Provenance (patch v1.1-3).} The rectangle theorem is imported from
operator verdicts \#9/\#10, archived in the folder note
\texttt{rectangle-constant-closure-260605-260605-v1.3.tex.txt} \S2--\S3:
verdict \#9 supplied the derivation $\sum_C p_C^3 \le 7N^3$ (carrier-circle
cube bound) with the Cauchy--Schwarz interpolation
$\sum_C p_C^2 \le (\sum_C p_C)^{1/2}(\sum_C p_C^3)^{1/2} \le \sqrt7\,N^{5/2}$;
verdict \#10 repaired the provisional incidence exponent to $20/9$ (correcting
the $28/13$ arithmetic slip). This package records and machine-reproduces the
numerical threshold but does not reprove the incidence alternative.

\section*{3. Structure of the argument and threshold derivation}

The rectangle prefactor is bounded by the closed-form
$8+4\sqrt{14}\,\kappa^4\sqrt{n}$ (the operator-verified rectangle theorem).
STEP-5B closes for a pattern of $n$ modes provided (patch v1.1-2)
\[
K(n) \;\le\; 8 + 4\sqrt{14}\,\kappa^4 \sqrt{n}
\;<\; K_{\rm budget}(I)
\;=\; \frac{4\,(1 - a_0(I))\,\Delta F_{\rm margin}}{(\lambda' I)^2 J_{\max}},
\]
which at the production anchor $I = 4\times10^{-4}$ (where
$K_{\rm budget} = 5972$, $\kappa \simeq 1$) gives the official admissible-mode
threshold
\[
n_{\rm adm}
\;\lesssim\;
\Bigl(\tfrac{K_{\rm budget} - 8}{4\sqrt{14}\,\kappa^4}\Bigr)^{2}
\;=\;
\Bigl(\tfrac{5972 - 8}{4\sqrt{14}}\Bigr)^{2}
\;=\; 1.59\times10^{5}.
\]
($3.77\times10^3$ at $I = 10^{-3}$; $2.02\times10^2$ at $I = 2\times10^{-3}$.)
The incidence-geometry alternative would relax this to $2.2\times10^{10}$ but
its constant is not pinned, so it is documented for cross-check only and is not
used in the closure.
