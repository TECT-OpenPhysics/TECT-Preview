\section*{1. Purpose and scope}

DR-2 is the unrestricted-class sphere additive-energy bound: for every finite
$Q\subset S^2$, $E_+(Q)\le C\,N^2\log^B N$, the open route to closing STEP-5B
without the H-ADM-COH amended class. It is OPEN and off the critical path (B5 is
T5 PINNED-CLOSURE on the amended class; B1 is T6). The operator-supplied external
research (Math447--469) reduces DR-2 to a single named conjecture. This note
formalises that residual at honest tiers and registers one verified supporting
lemma. It changes no tier.

\section*{2. The cross-energy lemma (T7 PROVED) and a correction}

\textbf{Lemma.} Let $A\subset C_1$, $B\subset C_2$ be finite subsets of circles on
$S^2$. With $r_P(w)=\#\{(p,p')\in P^2:p-p'=w\}$, the off-diagonal cross energy
$E_{\rm off}(A,B):=E_+(A,B)-|A||B|$ satisfies
\[
E_{\rm off}(A,B)\;\le\;2\,\min\!\big(|A|^2-|A|,\ |B|^2-|B|\big),
\qquad
E_+(A,B)\;\le\;|A||B|+2\min(|A|^2,|B|^2)\;\le\;3|A||B|.
\]
\emph{Proof.} For $w\neq0$, a circle and its translate meet in at most two points,
so $r_P(w)\le2$. Since $E_+(A,B)=\sum_w r_A(w)r_B(-w)$ and the $w=0$ term is
$r_A(0)r_B(0)=|A||B|$,
\[
E_{\rm off}(A,B)=\sum_{w\neq0}r_A(w)r_B(-w)\le2\sum_{w\neq0}r_A(w)=2(|A|^2-|A|),
\]
and symmetrically with $B$. $\square$

\textbf{Correction (devil's-advocate, code-discipline).} The external Pass~4
states $E_+(A,B)\le2|A||B|$ by applying $r\le2$ to ALL $w$, including $w=0$ where
$r_P(0)=|P|\not\le2$. This is false for the full energy: the witness
$A=B=16$ equally-spaced great-circle points has $E_+=720>512=2|A||B|$. The
corrected bounds above hold: the same witness has $E_{\rm off}=464\le480=
2(|A|^2-|A|)$. The non-parallel hypothesis of Pass~4 is moreover NOT load-bearing
-- the $r\le2$ mechanism holds for parallel and non-parallel circles alike
(verified on all four configurations). The qualitative $E_+(A,B)=O(|A||B|)$ and
the reduction below are unaffected; only the constant $2\to3$ (full) changes, and
the cluster-decomposition uses $E_{\rm off}$ with constant $2$.

\section*{3. The Pencil Rigidity / PSM conjecture (T2 CONJECTURE)}

\textbf{Conjecture (Pair-Sum Surface Multiplicity).} There exist $C,B$ such that
for every finite $Q\subset S^2$ decomposed into its circle-rich part, the
non-structured circle-pair interaction obeys
\[
\sum_{s}\Big(\sum_{\alpha,\beta}r_{\alpha\beta}(s)\Big)^2\le C\,N^2\log^B N,
\]
where $r_{\alpha\beta}(s)$ counts pairs $(a\in C_\alpha,b\in C_\beta)$ with
$a+b=s$. Equivalently (Pencil Rigidity): rich circle pencils sharing a common
pair-sum surface have $O(\log^B N)$ controlled overlap.

\textbf{Pre-registered falsification gate.} The conjecture (hence DR-2) is
FALSIFIED by any explicit family $Q_N\subset S^2$ with
$E_+(Q_N)\ge N^{2+\delta}$ for a fixed $\delta>0$ (super-polylogarithmic additive
energy). The external research's Pass-27 counterexample audit and the
Pass-9 no-small-doubling probe found no such family; the single-circle ($K=14$,
R-002), coaxial ($K\le30$), and rectangle ($R=O(n^{5/2})$, R-007) structured
classes are all $O(N^2)$ or better, consistent with the conjecture.

\section*{4. The reduction PSM $\Rightarrow$ DR-2 (T3 PROOF SKETCH)}

\textbf{Claim.} The PSM conjecture (equivalently Pencil Rigidity) implies DR-2.

\textbf{Sketch.} Decompose $Q=Q_{\rm thin}\cup Q_{\rm struct}\cup Q_{\rm rich}$.
(i) The circle-thin part obeys $E_+(Q_{\rm thin})\le L\,N^2$ with $L=\log^B N$
(few points per circle). (ii) The structured circle-rich part (single circles,
coaxial pencils) obeys $E_+(Q_{\rm struct})\le C N^2$ by the closed estimates
R-002/R-003/R-004/R-007. (iii) The remaining non-structured circle-rich
interaction is exactly the pair-sum surface multiplicity, bounded by $CN^2\log^B
N$ under PSM. (iv) Cross terms between the three parts are bounded by
Cauchy--Schwarz, $E_+(X,Y)\le\sqrt{E_+(X)E_+(Y)}$, and by the cross-energy lemma
of \S2 for circle pairs. Summation gives $E_+(Q)\le C'N^2\log^{B'}N$. $\square$

\textbf{Marked gaps (the T3 obstructions).} OPEN GAP $\alpha$: the existence of
the controlled decomposition into $O(\log^B N)$ structured clusters $+$ a thin
remainder is exactly the PSM/Pencil Rigidity conjecture (not independently
proved). OPEN GAP $\beta$: the uniformity of the per-cluster constants $C_\alpha$
across the dyadic richness scales (Pass-21) is asserted, not proved. These are
the residual obstructions that keep DR-2 at T1 OPEN and the reduction at T3.
