recomputed second-order floors $\rho = (\times59.4,\times8.8,\times2.6)$ at
$I = (4\times10^{-4}, 10^{-3}, 2\times10^{-3})$ and the paired joint inflation

\[

obstructed only at the $I = 2\times10^{-3}$ endpoint.

\textbf{Operator decision (2026-06-07):} accept second-cumulant scope at the
$I = 2\times10^{-3}$ endpoint. The all-orders lift is recorded as feasible for

two non-per-transfer routes (a sharper STEP-5B endpoint floor
