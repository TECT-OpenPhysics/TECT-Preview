\section*{1. Purpose and scope}

The AddA chain reduced STEP-5B closure to the named hypotheses H-KBAL
(lifted in principle, AddA v1.2) and H-ADM ($n \le n_{\rm adm}$ with the
verified threshold $n_{\rm adm} \lesssim 1.59\times10^5$ at the anchor).
This note derives a CANDIDATE value of $n_{\rm adm}$ from TECT
microphysics --- the coherence resolution of the dressed propagator ---

\section*{2. Derivation}

The dressed quadratic kernel at the production anchor is
$D(k) = \hat r + C(k^2 - q_0^2)^2$ with $\hat r = r_R + 2\lambda' I$.
At $I = 4\times10^{-4}$: $\hat r = 0.3110$ and
\[
\frac{\hat r}{C q_0^4} = 1.45 ,
\]
the STRONGLY DRESSED regime: the propagator is barely shell-peaked.
Expanding about the shell, $D \approx \hat r + 4Cq_0^2 (k - q_0)^2$, the
correlation length and the induced angular resolution are
\[
\xi \;=\; 2 q_0 \sqrt{C/\hat r} \;=\; 2.44 ,
\qquad
\theta_{\min} \;=\; \frac{1}{q_0 \xi} \;=\; \frac{\sqrt{\hat r}}{2 q_0^2 \sqrt C}
\;=\; 0.603\ \text{rad} .
\]
Two shell modes separated by an angle $\theta < \theta_{\min}$ produce a
beat wavevector $q_0\theta < 1/\xi$ whose phase cannot be resolved within
one correlation volume: within the Gaussian--Hartree variational class
they are not independent coherent READINGS but a single reading plus
sea-type fluctuation, which the layer (hypothesis H-LAYER) already
accounts for. Cap packing on the shell then caps the independent-reading
count:
\[
n_{\rm adm} \;\simeq\; c_{\rm geo}\,\frac{4\pi}{\theta_{\min}^2}
\;=\; c_{\rm geo}\,(q_0\xi)^2\,4\pi/(4\pi) \cdot \ldots
\;\approx\; 35 \quad (c_{\rm geo} = 1);
\qquad
\le 140 \quad (\times4\ \text{conservative}).
\]
The cutoff is nearly intensity-independent ($35/34/32$ at
$I = 4{\times}10^{-4}/10^{-3}/2{\times}10^{-3}$) because $\hat r$ varies
mildly across the anchor band.

\[
K(4 n_{\rm adm}) \approx 184 \;<\; K_{\rm budget}(I)
= 5972 / 927 / 221
\quad\text{at } I = 4{\times}10^{-4} / 10^{-3} / 2{\times}10^{-3},

therefore CLOSES at all anchor intensities under H-ADM-COH, with the

\section*{6. Devil's-advocate (three objections; CLAUDE.md 6.3)}

\begin{itemize}
\item[($\alpha$)] \emph{``The criterion is heuristic: beats below $1/\xi$
are suppressed, not forbidden.''} VALID-with-mitigation: this is why the
note is T3 and an AMENDMENT PROPOSAL, not a theorem. The rigorous form
(free-energy indistinguishability: sub-resolution mode splittings change
the variational energy by less than the sea-sector accounting) is the
registered follow-up; the $\times4$ packing band absorbs criterion
ambiguity at the production anchor ($\times32$ margin).
\item[($\beta$)] \emph{``$\xi$ uses the parabolic expansion, but the
shell is broad ($\hat r \sim Cq_0^4$).''} VALID-with-mitigation: the
broad shell weakens the parabolic approximation radially, but the
angular-resolution argument uses only the transverse beat scale
$1/\xi$, and a broader shell can only SHORTEN the true coherence range
(more soft modes to dephase against), making the quoted $n_{\rm adm}$
conservative in the relevant direction. An exact linewidth-integral
criterion is registered as the refinement, and the $I$-insensitivity of
the result ($35/34/32$) indicates robustness.
\item[($\gamma$)] \emph{``Circular dependence: $\hat r$ contains
$\lambda' I$, the quantity under study.''} DISMISSED: $\hat r$ is the
DRESSED kernel fixed by the anchor calibration (Math434 chain); the
cutoff is evaluated at each anchor intensity separately and varies by
$< 10\%$ across the band.
\item[($\delta$)] \emph{``The $I = 2\times10^{-3}$ margin ($\times1.2$)
is too thin to quote closure.''} VALID-with-mitigation: quoted honestly
as THIN; at $c_{\rm geo} = 1$ the ratio is $\times2.4$; the de-thinning
refinement is registered, and the production anchor itself has
$\times32$.
\end{itemize}
