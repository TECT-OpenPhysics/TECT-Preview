\section*{2. The M-ENDPOINT constant}

In the production convention $\hat r(I) = r_R + 2\lambda' I$ with
$r_R = $ gap point at $\mu^2 = 0.005$, $M_R = M(r_R)$, $\lambda' = 3u + 30vM_R$,
the dressing variance is the radial integral
\[
M(m) = \frac{1}{2\pi^2}\int_0^\infty \frac{k^2}{m + C(k^2 - q_0^2)^2}\,dk .
\]
Two independent quadratures (the production \texttt{M\_fast} and a direct radial
trapezoid) agree to $0.20\%$:
\[
\boxed{\,M\text{-ENDPOINT} = M(0.33675) = 0.104953\,}
\qquad (M_R = 0.109414,\ \hat r(2\times10^{-3}) = 0.33675).
\]
The value sits below $M_R$ (the dressing at the higher endpoint mass lowers the
variance, as monotonicity requires) and inside the corrected convex/secant
bracket $[0.10474, 0.10941]$ (the old $[0.1001, 0.1094]$ used the factor-2
slope). $M(0.32064) = 0.107098$ is the $I = 10^{-3}$ companion.

\section*{3. The corrected sunset-axis endpoint sign}

With $u_{\rm eff}(M) = u + 10vM$, the matched single-$J_0$ sunset bound and the
composed AddE margin are
\[
b(I) = \tfrac12\,u_{\rm eff}(M)^2\,(2I)\cdot 6 J_0\,M ,
\qquad
\mathrm{composed}(I) = \mathrm{MARGIN}\,\bigl(1 - 1/\rho(I)\bigr) ,

The frozen-coupling bound (U4) freezes $u_{\rm eff}$ at $M_R$; the physically
correct endpoint dresses each competitor at its OWN kernel $M(\hat r(I))$:
\[
\frac{\mathrm{composed}}{b}\Big|_{I = 2\times10^{-3}} :
\quad
\underbrace{\times0.970}_{\text{frozen } M_R\ (=\text{U4})}
\;\longrightarrow\;
\boxed{\,\underbrace{\times1.13}_{\text{dressed } M\text{-ENDPOINT}}\,} > 1 .

directly-evaluated M-ENDPOINT, the sunset-axis endpoint is POSITIVE: the U4
