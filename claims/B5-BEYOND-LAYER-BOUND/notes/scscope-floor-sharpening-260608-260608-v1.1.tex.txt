\section*{2. The floor composition}

The de-thinned STEP-5B closure floor (robustness\_mu2\_step5b\_remargin) is
\[
\rho=\frac{K_{\rm budget}}{K(n_{\rm pack})},\qquad
K_{\rm budget}=\frac{4(1-a_0)\,m}{(\lambda'I)^2 J_{\rm eff}},\qquad
K(n_{\rm pack})=8+4\sqrt{14}\,\sqrt{n_{\rm pack}},
\]
where $K(n_{\rm pack})$ is the kappa-balanced additive-energy bound and
$n_{\rm pack}=16/\theta_{\min}^2$. At the endpoint (anchor $\mu^2=0.005$):
$n_{\rm pack}=40.7$, $K(n_{\rm pack})=103.5$, $K_{\rm budget}=266.7$, so
$\rho=2.58$ (reproducing the AddE $\times2.6$).

\section*{3. The bound is loose at the small endpoint}

$K(n)=8+c_R\sqrt n$ with $c_R=4\sqrt{14}\approx15$ is the UNCONDITIONAL
triple-count bound ($E_+\le n^{5/2}\Rightarrow K=E_+/n^2\le\sqrt n$ up to the
prefactor). At the endpoint $n_{\rm pack}=40.7$ this gives $103.5$, which
EXCEEDS the trivial Lemma-A additive-energy bound
\[
\frac{E_+(Q)}{N^2}\le 1+T'(Q),\qquad T'(Q)\le|Q|\le n_{\rm pack}=40.7
\quad\Rightarrow\quad 1+T'\le41.7<103.5 .
\]
The large prefactor $c_R\approx15$ makes the $\sqrt n$ bound overshoot the trivial
$O(n)$ bound at this moderate $n$. The additive-energy constant $K$ and $1+T'$
are the SAME $\lambda'$-free quantity $(\langle F^4\rangle-4I^2)/I^2$ (via
$\sum w^2=\lambda'^2(\langle F^4\rangle-4I^2)$ and $\sum w^2\le K(\lambda'I)^2$),
so $1+T'$ is a legitimate, tighter substitute.
